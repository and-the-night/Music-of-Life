Music of Life

"The Music of Life" reimagines the popular "Game of Life" in a musical context (read more about the "Game of Life" here). This experiment assigns a note to each cell that plays if it's "alive" and silences if it's "dead". The grid moves in circle of fifths rightwards, and a reverse circle of fifths downwards (read more about the circle of fifths here). This experiment exposes the mathematical relationships between different notes on the chromatic scale. To play - click on each cell to activate it, then press "Play" to run the app and listen how a simple shape can easily become a post-modern, Schoenberg-like composition. Click "Reset" to clear the grid.
